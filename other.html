145 Axis

Biography

145 Axis is a band in the greater Salt Lake Area. They specialize in Rock music. They met through a church group and through a common friend. Learn about each of the individual band members by clicking here.

The band formed in 2023. They started out doing covers of other songs. Their first gig was in December 2023 at a church event. Following which, they began to write music. In February 2024, they recorded their first original: "Come On Baby". They debuted the song at battle of the bands in March 2024. They are currently working on an album.
